Title: Laptop Troubleshooting Guide

Employees experiencing laptop issues should follow these steps before contacting IT.

Common Laptop Issues:

Laptop Not Turning On
- Check if the charger is connected
- Hold the power button for 10 seconds
- Try a different power outlet

WiFi Not Working
- Restart the laptop
- Turn WiFi off and on
- Ensure airplane mode is disabled

Application Crashes
- Restart the application
- Restart the system
- Check for software updates

If the issue persists, contact the IT support team.

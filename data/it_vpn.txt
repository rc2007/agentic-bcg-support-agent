Title: VPN Troubleshooting Guide

Employees must connect to the corporate VPN when accessing internal resources remotely.

Common VPN Issues:

1. VPN Not Connecting
- Ensure internet connection is active
- Restart the VPN client
- Check username and password
- Update the VPN client to the latest version

2. Authentication Failed
- Verify corporate credentials
- Reset password through the IT portal if necessary

3. Slow VPN Connection
- Disconnect and reconnect VPN
- Switch to a stable network
- Avoid public WiFi if possible

If the problem continues, employees should contact the IT Service Desk or raise a support ticket.

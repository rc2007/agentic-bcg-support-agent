Title: Expense Reimbursement Policy

Employees can claim reimbursement for business-related expenses.

Eligible Expenses:
- Travel expenses
- Client meals
- Conference registration fees
- Office supplies

Reimbursement Process:
1. Upload receipts to the expense portal
2. Submit expense form
3. Manager approval required
4. Finance team reviews request

Reimbursements are typically processed within 7 business days.

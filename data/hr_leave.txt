
Title: Employee Leave Policy

Full-time employees are entitled to the following leave benefits:

Annual Leave
- 20 days per year
- Must be approved by the reporting manager

Sick Leave
- 10 days per year
- Medical documentation may be required for absences longer than 2 days

Emergency Leave
- Available for family emergencies
- Requires manager notification

Employees must submit leave requests through the HR Portal at least 3 days in advance for planned leave.
